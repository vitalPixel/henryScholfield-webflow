about

film director

Henry Scholfield is an award winning director that has won UK music video awards for best pop video - uk, best director, video of the year, and has worked with some of the most interesting people in the music and film industries.

Music videos

He has directed videos for Ed Sheehan, Dua Lipa, Billie Eilish, Stormzy, Ella Eyre, and many others. His music videos have had the participation of incredibly talented photographers, producers, actors, and many other professionals in the film industry.

Commercials

Henry was the creative drive behind some incredibly successful advertising campaigns for Samsung, Axe, Expedia, Doritos, Converse, and other world renowned brands.
